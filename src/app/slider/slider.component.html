Be Open to It

When trying something new for the first time. It can be intimidating. The whole process of learning something new is about figuring out the unknown. The unknown is something that is scary for lots of people. But, it doesn't need to be that way. Just being rationally seeing the possibility of something positive happening from the experience of trying to do something new can be exhilerating and rewarding.
Everyone gets more out of any experience by embracing it and giving it a shot. Instead of doing something without conviction or will to succeed. If belief isn't there, just taking actions towards the goal will create belief.

Story: Med School

There was a young woman I met at the grocery. She worked their part-time while going to community college full-time. She told when she was a teenager she would say to people she wanted to become a doctor. Everyone around her would say how difficult the whole process can be like it was something beyond her intelligence or ability. She later started to start listening to the people that doubted her ability. She decided not to go to college.
A couple years later, she decided to give it a shot. She became open to the possibility of going to school and finishing. She would tell me how it was so much easier than she thought it was going to be. She loved studying biology and the material was not much of a challenge.
